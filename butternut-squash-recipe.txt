1 Butternut Squash
1.5 cups lemon juice
5 garlic cloves
tea spoon salt
1 Sweet onion

Chop onion into strings
Chop butternut squash into half-moons

Steam onion for 2-4 minutes
Add butternut squash and steam for 20 minutes (add water as needed)

Make sure the squash is soft and add 1.5 cups lemons juice
half cup water
mashed garlic cloves
salt

Steam for 10 minutes and serve over rice. 

Enjoy!
